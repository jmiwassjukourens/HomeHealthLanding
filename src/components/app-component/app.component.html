What is
Home Health Care?

Home Health Care provides Medical and Personal Care Services in the Comfort of your Loved One’s Home.

At Los Angeles Senior Services, we help seniors stay Safe, Independent, an Well-Cared for with:



  • Skilled Nursing - Medication management, wound care & health monitoring.

  • Physical, Occupational & Speech Therapy – Support for mobility, strength & recovery.

  • Personal Care Assistance – Help with bathing, dressing & daily tasks.

  • Chronic Disease Management – Care for diabetes, heart disease, stroke recovery & more.

  • Fall Prevention & Safety Checks – Ensuring a secure home environment.

Who can Benefit for
Home Health Care?

Home Health Care Services are paid by Medicare. No Out-of-Pocket Costs are required to Enrol. Our Services are Designated for
Seniors Who:



  • ✅ Are recovering from illness, surgery, or hospitalization.

  • ✅ Need help managing chronic conditions like diabetes or heart disease.

  • ✅ Have mobility challenges and need assistance with daily activities.

  • ✅ Prefer to receive care at home rather than in a facility.

Not sure if your loved one qualifies?
